Future Directions

The field continues to evolve with the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning, which aid in data analysis, drug design, and personalized medicine approaches. Synthetic biology is also expanding possibilities by engineering biological systems to produce new therapies, vaccines, and biofuels. As our understanding of molecular processes deepens, cellular and molecular biology research will continue to drive medical breakthroughs and improve global health.
